Embedded Applications Technology Society

We are a project-based student organization whose focus is to participate in national and international competitions. We are primarily composed of Computer Engineering majors, however, we accept members from all majors. The only requirement to be a member of EAT is to be a member of California State University, Long Beach, and sign up online or in person at the meetings.

It is the purpose of this organization to introduce students to new technologies through various personal or group projects.
